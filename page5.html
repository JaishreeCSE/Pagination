The Learner Ben Tennyson:

Throughout his journey, Ben evolves from a carefree kid into a more mature and responsible hero. He faces moral dilemmas that challenge his understanding of right and wrong, prompting him to reflect on his actions. Each encounter teaches him valuable lessons about empathy, sacrifice, and humility. He learns that with great power comes great responsibility, shaping him into a wiser individual. Ben's journey is not just about fighting villains, but also about personal growth and understanding his place in the world.
