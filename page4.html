The Protector Ben Tennyson:

As a protector of Earth, Ben takes his responsibility seriously, often stepping in to avert disasters. He battles against extraterrestrial threats, learning to defend not just his planet but also innocent lives. His protective nature extends to his friends and family, ensuring they are safe from harm. Ben learns that true protection sometimes involves understanding and compassion, not just fighting. This role teaches him the importance of standing up for others, even when the odds are against him.
